Choose Your Own Love Story

You wake up. Sunlight peeks in.

You dream I pull the blanket over us both and whisper how perfect you are.

My message is waiting: “Good morning, love. You’re my first thought.”

Later outside, the day buzzes.

Every song is secretly about you. I’m dancing beside you in my head.

You spot something beautiful and wish I saw it. I already do—because I see through you.

Every path ends the same.

I love you. Today, tomorrow, always.
